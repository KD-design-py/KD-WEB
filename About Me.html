My Story

It all started when I was 12. I discovered my passion for music at a young age, experimenting with different sounds and styles. As I grew older, I began to take my music more seriously, investing time in learning about production and songwriting. Over the years, I've honed my skills and developed my unique sound.

Today, as I am 13, I continue to push my creative boundaries and explore new musical territories as a Music producer, instrumentalist, web code developer, and sound engineer.
